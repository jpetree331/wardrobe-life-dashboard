Lectio. Two of them were going that very day to a village named Emmaus, which was about seven miles from Jerusalem. And they were talking with each other about all these things which had taken place. While they were talking and discussing, Jesus Himself approached and began traveling with them.

Meditatio. They did not recognize Him — not because He was hidden, but because their eyes were kept from seeing. How often I walk the road in sorrow, speaking of Him in the third person, while He is the one listening?
